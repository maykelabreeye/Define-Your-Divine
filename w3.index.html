About

Define Your Divine you will find a community of power players, jumping back into the game of life and declaring to the world, "We will NOT be counted out!". These amazing individuals each bring a special perspective and solution to what makes them unique. At Define Your Divine, we are in the business of defining ourselves. I created this community to share what I have learned throughout my journey of transformation with you. My hope is that you will resonate deeper with your own unique transformations, and from them birth a life that is completely new to you. I hope to help you unlock the kind of impact that reminds you, that you are capable of defining and redefining yourself, as many times as you feel empowered. The world needs more game-changers, wiling to share their experiences through elevated voices and motivational messages. Here, at Define Your Divine (self), we believe in YOU!
